Are Aromiq BD perfumes authentic and long-lasting?

We hand-pour inspired blends that stay faithful to the original fragrance DNA. Expect 6–10 hours of wear on average when applied to pulse points and allowed to dry down properly.

What is the best way to store perfume oils for longevity?

Keep each bottle upright in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Tighten the cap after every use—proper storage preserves top notes.

How fast do you process and deliver orders in Bangladesh?

Orders confirmed via Instagram, TikTok, Facebook (@aromiqbd) or WhatsApp are packed within 24 hours. Nationwide courier delivery typically takes 3–5 business days depending on your city.
